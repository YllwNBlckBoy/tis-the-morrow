The Time Collapse Zine

Intention Note...

I created this product and continue to create products to better myself. And, generally, I feel nothing towards possession/ownership, copies, and replicas. As long as I benefit from my work, we all can. So, I chose the Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 4.0 International License because it goes with my belief that society cannot move forward if we all are not moving forward.

And with that said, I have a lot of work to do... a lot of things to write, a lot of things to build better for the sake of making people want to reuse it all.

Description

The Time Collapse is a digital zine that proved to be quite difficult to finish. Mainly because of the stress of time and it's release is it's own accomplishment. Another zine for sale means a lot when getting things done is hard...harder the longer it takes...painful if your mind wonders away. So...this zine is just me, trying to create something meaningful in a collapsing state--working towards something and failing, and then keep trying toughen through it, and then realizing you're not strong enough. You're not strong enough and you don't know why.
